# Fractional Hermite Theory in the Complex Domain

## Abstract
This page presents my research on fractional Hermite theory in the complex domain. The work focuses on the definition, representation, and properties of complex fractional Hermite objects, together with their connections to Laguerre-type functions. I also study orthogonality, completeness, and Wiener-type expansions on a finite disk, aiming to build a systematic theoretical framework.
